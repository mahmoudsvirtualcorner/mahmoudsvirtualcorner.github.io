Short Bio

I recently defended my Ph.D. in December 2025 under the supervision of Prof. Dr.-Ing. Carsten Dachsbacher . I previously earned my M.Sc. (2011) and B.Sc. (2004) degrees from the Department of Computer Science, Faculty of Computer and Information Sciences, Ain Shams University, Egypt.

In Spring 2026, I will be co-teaching Computer Graphics at the Faculty of Computer and Information Sciences, Ain Shams University, together with Dr. Salsabil Elregaily.
